### Product pages

#### Control Definition
- Create, assign, and version controls with structured metadata: owner, reviewer, frequency, success criteria, mapped frameworks, and attached procedures.
- Track implementation status and coverage across services and teams.

#### Evidence Repository
- Automated collection from integrations or manual uploads with templates.
- Tagging, deduplication, versioning, and retention policies.
- Review & approval workflows with timestamps and audit logs.

#### Violation Queue
- Real‑time detection feeds prioritized queues.
- Assign owners, set due dates/SLAs, comment threads, and link remediation tasks in Jira/ServiceNow.
- Custom rules to auto‑route or suppress known exceptions.

#### Notifications
- Subscriptions for control owners and auditors.
- Alerts for expiring evidence, missed reviews, and new violations.
- Digest summaries and channel delivery (email, Slack/Teams).

#### Dashboard & Reporting
- Posture score by framework and domain.
- Trends for evidence freshness, violation MTTR, and control coverage.
- One‑click exports for auditors and leadership.

#### User & Role Management
- SSO/SAML/OIDC, SCIM provisioning.
- Granular roles: Admin, Control Owner, Evidence Reviewer, Auditor (read‑only), Contributor.
- Immutable audit logs and IP allow‑listing.
